About Dr. Rachita Rangpal

Dr. Rachita Rangpal is a dedicated and compassionate professional in the field of Radio Diagnosis. She applies her comprehensive knowledge and skills to provide accurate and timely diagnostic insights.

She graduated with an MBBS from the prestigious Christian Medical College (CMC), Vellore, where she developed a strong foundation in medical sciences and patient care. Her academic journey has equipped her with a deep understanding of various imaging modalities, including X-rays, Ultrasound, CT scans, and MRI.

Dr. Rachita is committed to continuous learning and staying abreast of the latest advancements in radiological science. She believes in a patient-centric approach, ensuring clear communication of findings and collaborating closely with referring physicians to provide holistic care, aiming for the best possible outcomes for her patients.

Our Comprehensive Diagnostic Services

X-ray Imaging

High-quality digital X-rays for skeletal, chest, and abdominal diagnostics.

Ultrasound Scans

Detailed ultrasound examinations for abdominal, pelvic, obstetric, and soft tissue areas.

CT Scans

Advanced Computed Tomography for precise internal imaging of various body parts.

MRI Scans

Magnetic Resonance Imaging for detailed insights into neurological, musculoskeletal, and other complex conditions.

Doppler Studies

Non-invasive ultrasound for evaluating blood flow and detecting vascular conditions.

Interventional Radiology

Minimally invasive image-guided procedures for diagnosis and treatment.
